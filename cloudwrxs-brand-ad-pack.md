## Source Positioning

CloudWRXS positions itself as an AI, SAP, Oracle, and cloud modernization specialist, with IT economics at the heart of business change. Core proof points used in this pack:

- SAP and Oracle migrations to cloud
- AWS Advanced Consulting Partner positioning
- AWS SAP Competency and AWS Well-Architected Review capability
- Oracle, SAP, AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, Alibaba, and OCI ecosystem experience
- Business-led discovery, planning, migration, and managed services
- Cost transparency, resilience, security, and operational control

## Creative Notes

- Lead with business outcomes, not just platform capability.
- Use "IT economics" as the brand-level differentiator.
- Use "modernize", "assess", "roadmap", "cost clarity", "resilience", and "control" repeatedly across campaigns.
- Keep platform-specific ads tightly mapped to landing pages. Avoid sending SAP or Oracle searches to the generic homepage.
- For MENA campaigns, test bilingual English/Arabic variants and region-specific CTAs such as "Book a strategy session" or "Arrange a private workshop".
