Campaign: SAP Modernisation, SAP Migration, SAP Cost Optimisation, S/4HANA Readiness
1. Campaign Overview
The purpose of this campaign is to operationalise Cloudwrxs’ new position as an AWS SAP Competency Partner, generating qualified SAP opportunities in KSA by focusing on:
* SAP-to-AWS Migrations

* SAP Modernisation & Performance Optimisation

* S/4HANA Readiness Assessments

* SAP TCO Reduction & Financial Optimisation

* SAP HA/DR Redesign

* SAP Security & Zero Trust

* SAP Operations Automation

The campaign targets CFOs, CTOs/Heads of IT, and IT Managers, using email cadences, WhatsApp follow-ups, LinkedIn content, landing pages, and structured call scripts.
2. Campaign Timeline (January–March 2026)
The campaign is structured into three monthly waves, each containing four email drops, continuous outreach, one major webinar, and multiple reinforcement activities.
