### Section 2: The AWS Well-Architected Framework Explained (12 minutes)
**Speaker:** Principal Cloud Architect

**Pillar-by-Pillar Overview (2 minutes each):**

**Operational Excellence:**
- Infrastructure-as-Code and deployment automation
- Monitoring and observability with CloudWatch, X-Ray
- Automated incident response and runbooks
- Continuous improvement cycles
- Key metric: Deployment frequency and MTTR

**Security:**
- Zero Trust architecture and IAM best practices
- Automated threat detection with GuardDuty
- Compliance automation for SAMA, NCA, GDPR
- Encryption everywhere: at rest, in transit, in processing
- Key metric: Security incidents per quarter

**Reliability:**
- Multi-AZ and cross-region design patterns
- Automated disaster recovery and failover
- Chaos engineering and resilience testing
- Health checks and circuit breaker patterns
- Key metric: Uptime percentage and RTO/RPO

**Performance Efficiency:**
- Right-sizing and instance selection
- Caching strategies with CloudFront and ElastiCache
- Auto-scaling for dynamic workloads
- Database optimization and selection
- Key metric: Response time and throughput

**Cost Optimization:**
- FinOps practices and cost governance
- Rightsizing, Reserved Instances, Savings Plans
- Automated resource scheduling
- Tagging and cost attribution
- Key metric: Cost per transaction/user

**Sustainability:**
- Graviton instances for energy efficiency
- Serverless and managed services adoption
- Data lifecycle management
- Carbon footprint measurement
- Key metric: Carbon emissions per workload
