Windows EC2 SDP Campaign 2 — Animated Video Script
"Why Move Windows to AWS with SDP" — 60-Second Explainer

**Video Concept:** Professional animated explainer targeting KSA/MENA business decision-makers showcasing Windows Server migration benefits through AWS Software Discount Program (SDP).

**Visual Style:** Clean, corporate animation with Saudi business context. Professional Arabic-English bilingual approach with modern graphics and data visualisations.

**Target Audience:** IT Operations Managers, Procurement/Vendor Managers, CTOs, CFOs in KSA/MENA region.

**Key Messages:** Licensing savings, performance improvement, security enhancement, operational simplification.

## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Visual Design Guidelines:
**Color Palette:**
- Primary: AWS Orange (#FF9900), Cloudwrxs Blue
- Secondary: Professional grays, whites for clarity
- Accent: Saudi green (#006C35) for cultural relevance
- Charts/Data: Contrasting colors for accessibility

**Typography:**
- Arabic: Professional Arabic font supporting both languages
- English: Clean, corporate sans-serif (Arial, Calibri, or similar)
- Hierarchy: Large headings, readable body text, clear CTAs

**Animation Style:**
- Smooth, professional transitions
- Clean geometric shapes and corporate iconography
- Data visualization with animated counters and charts
- Subtle motion graphics maintaining professional tone

### Audio Specifications:
**English Narration:**
- Clear, professional British or neutral English accent
- Moderate pace allowing for technical concept absorption
- Authoritative yet approachable tone for business decision-makers

**Arabic Narration (Optional Track):**
- Professional Modern Standard Arabic (MSA)
- Clear pronunciation for business terminology
- Cultural sensitivity in business language usage

**Background Music:**
- Subtle, corporate-style instrumental
- Non-distracting volume allowing narration clarity
- Positive, forward-moving tone supporting transformation message

### Technical Delivery:
**Video Formats:**
- Primary: MP4 H.264 for web compatibility
- Dimensions: 1920x1080 (Full HD) for professional presentation
- Frame Rate: 30fps for smooth animation
- File Size: Optimized for web streaming (under 50MB)

**Audio Quality:**
- Clear narration recording without background noise
- Balanced audio levels between narration and background music
- Professional voice recording with appropriate business tone

### Cultural Considerations:
**Regional Appropriateness:**
- Business imagery reflecting KSA/MENA professional environment
- Cultural sensitivity in visual representation of people
- Appropriate business dress and meeting contexts
- Respectful integration of regional business practices

**Language Accessibility:**
- English as primary with optional Arabic subtitles
- Technical terms clearly explained for non-technical decision-makers
- Business terminology appropriate for executive audiences
- Clear pronunciation of company and technical names
