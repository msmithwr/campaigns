# Campaign: Windows EC2 SDP Campaign 1

## 1. Campaign Overview

Windows EC2 SDP Campaign 1 is now a one-month campaign focused on Windows Infrastructure Awareness & Cost Challenges. It uses the first-month wave from the original Windows modernisation campaign and runs in May 2026.

## 2. Campaign Timeline (May 2026)

The campaign contains one monthly wave with landing page activation, persona segmentation, three email drops, WhatsApp follow-ups, sales calls, and workshop conversion.

## 3. High-Level Monthly Theme

| Month | Theme | Primary CTA |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| May 2026 | Windows Infrastructure Awareness & Cost Challenges | Free Windows Estate Assessment |

## 4. Detailed Campaign Calendar (Weekly Breakdown)

### MAY 2026 – WINDOWS INFRASTRUCTURE AWARENESS & COST CHALLENGES

#### Week 1 (1–7 May)
**Activities:**
* Launch campaign landing pages (CTO, CFO, IT Manager targeting Windows challenges)
* Begin persona segmentation and Windows-focused email list refinement
* Soft outreach to key Windows-heavy accounts in KSA/MENA

**Deliverables Used:**
* Landing Pages for Windows Estate Assessment
* CTA: Free Windows Estate Assessment Workshop

#### Week 2 (8–14 May)
**Outbound:**
* Email 1 to all personas (Windows infrastructure challenges + benefits of Windows-on-AWS with SDP)

**Sales Activities:**
* Stage 1 Calls using Windows-focused scripts
* Qualify early Windows modernisation opportunities

#### Week 3 (15–21 May)
**Outbound:**
* Email 2 to all personas (Windows SDP deep dive: cost savings, performance, architecture)
* WhatsApp Message 2

**Sales Activities:**
* Stage 2 Calls using Windows call scripts

#### Week 4 (22–31 May)
**Outbound:**

* WhatsApp Message 3
